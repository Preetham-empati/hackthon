About Us

Trash to Treasure is a platform designed to promote sustainability by connecting individuals to swap their unused items. Inspired by the barter system, we aim to reduce waste, encourage reusability, and foster a community of eco-conscious users.

Our mission is to combat pollution and overconsumption by offering a user-friendly, C-to-C (consumer-to-consumer) swapping network. Unlike traditional marketplaces like eBay or OLX, we focus solely on swappingâ€”no money changes hands, just goods.
